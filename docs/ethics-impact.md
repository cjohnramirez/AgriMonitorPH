# Ethics and Social Impact Statement

## Stakeholders
* **Primary:** Local farmers in Mindanao/CDO (data consumers), Agri-tech developers.
* **Secondary:** Government agricultural agencies (PSA/NFA), Commodity traders, USTP Academic Faculty.

## Potential Harms & Mitigations
| Potential Harm | Mitigation Strategy |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Market Manipulation** | Ensure data is sourced from verified PSA/NFA reports with clear timestamps. |
| **Digital Divide** | Optimize the static site for low-bandwidth mobile devices used in rural areas. |
| **Misinterpretation of Trends** | Include clear disclaimers that "Outlook" data is for educational/demo use only. |
